Welcome to the T-Rex Runner!
The goal is to survive as long as possible. The way you do this is by avoiding the obsticles that move in front of you.
You can avoid them by jumping over them (using the space bar). However, if you do run into an object, the game is over.
Click the spacebar to start, Good luck!
